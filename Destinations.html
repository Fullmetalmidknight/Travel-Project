Our Destinations

Paris, France "Bonjour"

The capital and most populous city of France. Located in western Europe, it is home to famous landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum, the Palace of the Versailles, the Arc De Triomphe, the Basilica of the Sacre-Coeur, the Champs-Elysées, and the Notre-Dame.

Stockholm, Sweden "Hej"

The capital and beautiful waterside city of Sweden. Located in the middle of Scandinavia, in Northern Europe, it is home to Gamla Stan, the Vasa Museum, Djurgarden Island, Stadshuset, Drottningholm Palace, and Kungliga Slottet.

Berlin, Germany "Hallo"

The capital of a once divided nation, located in Northwest Germany. Germany sits to the right of France and to the left of Poland, in central Europe. Berlin contains many artifacts of history like the Berlin Wall Memorial, the Holocaust Memorial, the Berliner Dome, the East Side Gallery, the Brandenburg Gate, the Reichstag Building, and Checkpoint Charlie.

Singapore, Singapore "Nín hǎo"

The city-state island in Southeast Asia. Located south of the Malaysian Peninsula, Singapore’s main attractions include the Marina Bay Sands, the Singapore Flyer, the Buddha Tooth Relic Temple, the Night Safari, the Singapore Botanic Gardens, the Gardens By the Bay, Resorts World Sentosa, and Orchard Road.

Sydney, Australia "Hello"

The gorgeous waterfront capital of Australia, located in the Southeast part of the country. Australia lies south of Indonesia and west of New Zealand. Some of the best places to visit in Sydney include the Sydney Opera House, Bondi Beach, the Queen Victoria Building, the Sydney Tower, the Sydney Harbour Bridge, and the Royal Botanic Gardens.

Tokyo, Japan "Kon'nichiwa"

The bustling capital of Japan. Just like its neighbors China and Korea, Japan is a densely populated and urbanized country. Some of the top sights to see in Tokyo include Mount Fuji, the Atomic Bomb Dome, the Tokyo Skytree, the Imperial Palace, the Shibuya Crossing, the Tokyo Tower, and the Hachiko Statue.
